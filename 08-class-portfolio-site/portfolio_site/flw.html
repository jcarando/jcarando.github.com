Frank Lloyd Wright Research Booklet

Spring 2011

objective:

To create a hand-bound research booklet about an individual of our choice. As an avid fan of architect Frank Lloyd Wright, the choice seemed obvious.

The text is set in P22 FLLW Exhibition Bold (designed by FLW) & Centaur. The cover his own typeface converted into a stencil & then hand cut. I used a Japanese Stab Binding method which is both architectural in it's structure, & a nod to his Japanese woodblock print collection. The contents of the book summarize Wright's work and his chaotic personal life.

medium:

created in Adobe Indesign, hand-stitched & hand-cut cover
