Nepal Travel Poster & Trekking Schedule

Spring 2011

objective:

To create a pocket-sized schedule, with a type-based travel poster as its cover. I selected Nepal as the travel destination & paired the poster with an Everest Trekking schedule. On the front side of the schedule, location, time, incline, departure, & elevation are listed. On the back, each site's attractions are listed with a color field corresponding to the altitude.

medium:

hand-cut paper poster; vector schedule, created in Adobe Illustrator
